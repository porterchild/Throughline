## Key Concepts in Neural Robotic Visual Navigation\n- **PointGoal Navigation**: Navigate to a fixed (x,y) coordinate using egocentric sensors (RGB/D).\n- **ObjectNav/Object Goal Navigation**: Find and reach a category/instance specified by language/image.\n- **ImageGoal Navigation**: Navigate to location of a given goal image.\n- **Vision-Language Navigation (VLN/VLN-CE)**: Follow natural language instructions in discrete/continuous envs.\n- **Topological Maps**: Graphs with nodes (semantic features/images) + coarse edges; enable long-horizon planning without full metric maps.\n- **Neural SLAM**: End-to-end learned mapping/localization/planning, often graph-based with semantics.\n- **VLM Navigation**: Vision-Language Models (e.g., CLIP, LLaVA) for open-vocab semantic reasoning, language goals, zero-shot.\n\n## Benchmarks\n- **Habitat**: Gibson, Matterport3D (MP3D), HM3D; sim-to-real focus, ObjectNav/OVON.\n- **R2R-CE/RxR-CE**: Continuous VLN.\n- **AI2-THOR/ProcTHOR**: Interactive indoor.\n\n## Landscape\nField shifting from classical SLAM to neural/graph/topo for generalization; VLMs enable language/open-vocab; labs: CMU (Gupta/Chaplot topo SLAM), Meta Habitat (benchmarks/policies), emerging VLM (diverse), bio-outdoor (insect).
